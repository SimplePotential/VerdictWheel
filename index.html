VerdictWheel

Delegating your indecision to the spin

    How to use VerdictWheel

    Adding options

    Type an option label in the text field, set an optional weight (higher weight = more likely to win), then click Add or press Enter.

    Weights

    Each wedge occupies a slice proportional to its weight. An option with weight 2 is twice as likely to be chosen as one with weight 1.

    Spinning the wheel

    Click anywhere on the wheel to spin it. When it stops, the wedge under the red pointer is the winner — it will glow to confirm the result.

    Clearing & restarting

    Click Clear to remove all options.

    Random Set

    Click Random Set to instantly populate the wheel with a random selection of options — great for testing or inspiration.

    Sharing

    • Share (edit) — copies a link that lets others load your wheel and make changes.
    • Share (view) — copies a read-only link; viewers can spin but not edit. This does not completely prevent editing — it merely defaults the editing controls to hidden.

    Loading a saved wheel

    Paste a previously shared link or encoded string into the input field and click Load.

    You can also load a wheel directly by pasting a copied link into your browser's address bar and pressing Enter — no need to use the Load field at all. You can bookmark that URL to return to the same wheel any time.

    Maximum options

    You can add up to 50 options manually. The Random Set generator creates up to 15.
